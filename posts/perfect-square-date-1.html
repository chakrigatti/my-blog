Perfect Square Date - Part 1

Last may, I had noticed a pattern in the date and I liked it very much immediately. The date was 05/05/25. The year was a perfect square of the day and the month. When I looked back for those perfect square dates, I found many other dates like that. Even the gap between the square dates are in a odd number pattern.

3/3/9 4/4/16 - Previous square date 5/5/25 - Current square date 6/6/36 - Next square date 7/7/49

After seeing the gap between two consecutive square dates it made me contemplate what I was doing on the previous square dates.

I will continue in part 2 about how my thinking process was on the previous square date.
