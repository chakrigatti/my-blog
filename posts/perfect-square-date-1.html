Perfect Square Date - Part 1

Last May, I noticed a pattern in the date that immediately caught my attention. The date was 05/05/25. The year was the perfect square of the day and the month values.

When I looked back for similar perfect square dates,I found many others,and even discovered the gaps between the square dates follow an odd number pattern.

Perfect Square Date Pattern:

3/3/9
    ↓ 7 years
4/4/16 (Previous square date)
    ↓ 9 years
5/5/25 (Current square date)
    ↓ 11 years
6/6/36 (Next square date)
    ↓ 13 years
7/7/49

The gaps between the square dates are: 7, 9, 11, 13...

After seeing the gaps between consecutive square dates, I began to contemplate what I was doing during those previous milestone dates.

I will continue in Part 2 with my reflections and what I was doing on the previous square dates.
