First Blog Post

This is my first blog post, and I have been wanting to post this for a long time. I have read many blogs in hacker news and read many posts describing how writing helps in many ways. Also, I liked the idea of a personal website designed by me.

What do I expect from this blog?

I would like to write my opinions, experiences and things I learned. I want it to be mainly technical . I am not sure about what content I should post here. I will figure it out as I go. For now, I will just note down whatever I feel like is important. I think this could be the place where I can find things I learned related to technology. Currently, I am learning CSS so that I could design the blog myself according to the needs. I could summarise the tricks I used while learning some topic.

How do I want this blog in the future?

In future, I want this blog to have a slick design. I would also like to write some posts in my mother tongue. So in future, it should have an option to switch between languages and translate the post to a different language. Currently, it has only two views: one is the "Home" page and the other is the post. I want to add more options like about page, my works, etc. I also want to add tags to the posts. I have seen many small features in blogs like the time it will take to read the post, back to top option, etc. It should be a continuous development, including everything I liked while I came across reading other blog posts.

How will be the writing style?

It is hard to write a polished long post. We can easily dump our ideas onto paper, but it is hard to organize the content in a manner that others can understand.

Currently, I do not have any style, and it is a mess. I will improve it gradually. Currently I am just writing the monologue in my head. I am just talking to myself and writing those thoughts here.

How frequent will your posts be?

This is the most difficult question in this post. One post a month will be too long, and I will easily procrastinate and forget about this. One post a week is too short because I have to put a lot of effort to get ideas about the posts and write about them in the short period of time.  Like I said, it is hard to write a polished post.  

For now, I want to try writing one post per week and see how it goes. I will try to complete the post on the weekend and post it.

In conclusion, I will start writing from now onwards and try to keep it up every week.
